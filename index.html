Abdelhak Mahmoudi

IMIARF Laboratory, Ecole Normale Supèrieure, Mohammed V University, Rabat, Morocco. abdelhak.mahmoudi@um5.ac.ma
(+212)670-536-341

Abdelhak Mahmoudi is Associate Professor (Professeur Habilité) at the Department of Computer Science of Ecole Normale Supérieure (ENS-Rabat) of Mohammed V University. He is a researcher at Laboratoire d’Informatique, Mathématique Appliquée, Intelligence Artificielle et reconnaissance de Formes (LIMIARF) since 2007. In 2010, he was involved in the Neuromed project under the 7th Framework at Institut de Neurosciences de la Timone (INT) (Marseille, France) and joined, in 2014, as a Postdoc Researcher, a Partnerships for Enhanced Engagement in Research (PEER) project administered by the United State National Academy of Sciences (NAS). Abdelhak’s main research area is machine learning, and most of his work is in the deep learning applied to real world problems.
